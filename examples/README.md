## Why "reference only"

Two failure modes if you treat these as templates:

1. **Mimicry without fit** — Gary's 27 traits work for Gary because they came from Gary's source material (journal, recommendation letters, CliftonStrengths). Copying his trait list gives you Gary's portrait, not yours.
2. **Number anchoring** — "27" isn't a target. Your convergence may be 12 or 40. Anchoring on Gary's count distorts your own process.

The valuable transferable layer is the **6-step methodology**:

1. Brain dump from raw material
2. Layer scaffolding
3. External validation pass
4. Shadow pass
5. Focus principles distillation
6. Update discipline

That part reproduces. The specific numbers don't.
